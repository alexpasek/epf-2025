# Google Ads Landing Page Plan (Popcorn Ceiling Removal)

## Goal
Build 4 low-distraction, high-conversion landing pages for Google Ads:

- `/locations/popcorn-ceiling-removal-mississauga`
- `/locations/popcorn-ceiling-removal-oakville`
- `/locations/popcorn-ceiling-removal-burlington`
- `/locations/popcorn-ceiling-removal-waterdown`

## Primary Actions (only two)

- Call
- Get Quote (short form)

## Template Sections (fixed order)

1. Minimal header (logo + phone + optional "See Photos")
2. Hero with city H1 + benefit bullets + quote/call buttons + quick estimate card
3. Trust strip
4. How it works (4 steps)
5. Before/after gallery
6. Reviews (3 featured + 6 compact)
7. Services included (offer stack)
8. FAQ
9. Final CTA
10. Simple footer

## Conversion/UX Rules

- No full navigation menu
- No unrelated links
- Form above the fold
- Sticky mobile call + quote actions
- City-message match in URL, title, H1, intro

## Tracking Requirements

- Phone click event
- Quote CTA click event
- Form submit success event
- Optional `gtag` conversion hooks when available

## Assets Checklist (to keep improving conversion)

- 10-20 before/after photos
- 6-12 short, real reviews
- Phone number and service badges
- City-specific captions and local area mentions
